The Atelier

A quieter standard.

Maliki Atelier was founded as a considered response to the noise of the modern jewellery industry — a return to the practice of making one piece at a time, by hand, for a person whose name we know. Each piece is hand-finished in our London workshop, in metals we cast ourselves from recycled gold and platinum, and stones sourced through long-standing relationships in Antwerp, Bogotá, and Yangon.

△ Hand-finished in London

Every piece passes through three hands — goldsmith, setter, and polisher — before it leaves the bench.

△ Lifetime Polish & Inspection

We offer complimentary inspection and re-polish for the lifetime of any piece that leaves the atelier.

△ Insured White-Glove Delivery

All orders are dispatched by white-glove courier, fully insured, and signed for in person — worldwide.
